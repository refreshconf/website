Stephanie Nemeth

Haute Codeture

What if, instead going for practicality, we used IoT to create fleeting moments of interaction and beauty? Stephanie was curious to answer these questions when she tackled her latest hardware project. She will share her story of building interactive fashion, the obstacles she faced as an IoT newbie, and the solutions she found to overcome them.

Stephanie Nemeth

Stephanie is a developer living in Amsterdam. She works on frontend stuff at Werkspot and co-organizes the Stupid Hackathon Amsterdam. She enjoys experimenting with hardware and LEDs to make beautiful, useless things.
