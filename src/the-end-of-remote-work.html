Job van der Voort

The end of remote work

Remote work will no longer be a thing in the future. It’ll be like everything else, just: work. Why is this the case and how will we get there? What is stopping us?

In this talk, I’ll explain, question and challenge us to reconsider the way we think about work and life - and discuss some of the challenges of bringing the future closer.

Job van der Voort

Coming from neuroscience, Job went head-first into tech by starting a startup, later joining GitLab as VP of Product to help scale it from 5 people to a unicorn with 400+ employees. He’s now helping the world work remotely as CEO of Remote.com.

In his free time Job loves to spend time with his wife and daughter, and play games and guitar.
