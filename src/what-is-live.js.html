Jan Krutisch & Tim Pietrusky

What is {live:js}?

Have you ever wondered how the web can be used to connect people with each other in a way that involves live audio & visual experiences? {live:js} is a group of artists that tries to do this and more using a lot of JavaScript, but is expanding into other directions as well.

Jan Krutisch

Jan is an environmental engineer by training who has built web applications for more than 20 years for reasons not entirely clear to anyone.

He's based in Hamburg, Germany where he lives with a bunch of synthesizers, computers and a 3d printer.

He's a musician in {live:js} and creates tracks in all styles between techno and hip hop.

Tim Pietrusky

Tim lives with his lovely family directly next to a forest, co-founded Synoa to work as the technical lead, performs as a Digital Artist (aka NERDDISCO) around the world as part of {live:js} + schickmalzwo and does useless things with the Bullgit crew. His passion is to connect all the things with each other.
